Dr. Muhle attends the 2022 World Congress of Psychiatric Genetics (WCPG) in Florence, Italy.

WCPG is hosted annually by the International Society of Psychiatric Genetics (ISPG). [The ISPG](https://ispg.net/about-us/), established in 1992, *'...is a collaboration of physicians, researchers, geneticists, social workers, genetic counselors, students, or any professionals with interest in the field of psychiatric genetics.'*
