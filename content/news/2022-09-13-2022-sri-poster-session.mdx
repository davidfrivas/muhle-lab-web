Congratulations to Muhle Lab researchers Sam Dhanani, Esther Garfield, and Mahdia Begum for presenting their research projects at Barnard College's 2022 Summer Research Institute (SRI)!

SRI is a summer program offered by Barnard College for their students to conduct faculty-mentored research. Find out more about Barnard's SRI program [here](https://barnard.edu/summer-research-institute).
