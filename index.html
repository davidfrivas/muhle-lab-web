Neurodevelopment and Molecular Genomics for Autism Research

Columbia University Department of Psychiatry

Bridging the gap between molecular genetics and psychiatry

We aim to uncover genetic pathways that increase risk for neurodevelopmental disorders such as autism by utilizing high-throughput genomics methods in mouse and human model systems and examining the effects of gene disruptions.
