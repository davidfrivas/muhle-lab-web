13 September 2022

Dr. Muhle Attends WCPG in Florence

Dr. Muhle attends the 2022 World Congress of Psychiatric Genetics (WCPG) in Florence, Italy.

WCPG is hosted annually by the International Society of Psychiatric Genetics (ISPG). The ISPG, established in 1992, '...is a collaboration of physicians, researchers, geneticists, social workers, genetic counselors, students, or any professionals with interest in the field of psychiatric genetics.'
