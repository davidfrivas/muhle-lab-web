Alumni

Sam Dhanani

Research Assistant (2023-2025)

During her time in the lab, Sam studied the behavior and neural circuitry of Chd8 haploinsufficient mice. She pioneered assays such as operand conditioning and fiber photometry. Sam is currently a PhD student in Neuroscience and Behavior at University of Massachusetts Amherst.

Yun-Kyung Kim

Research Assistant (2020-2022)

During her time in the lab, Yun-Kyung studied the molecular biology of Chd8 haploinsufficiency in mice. She employed techniques such as chromatin immunoprecipitation (ChIP), RNA extraction, cDNA synthesis, qRT-PCR analysis, and western blotting. Additionally, she performed a comprehensive medical literature review of the association of likely damaging human CHD8 mutations with seizures and abnormal EEG. Yun-Kyung is currently a medical student at Wright State University Boonshoft School of Medicine.
