## Creating a News Post

### Step 1: Access News Posts

1. Go to `https://muhlelab.org/admin`
2. Log in with GitHub
3. Click **"News Posts"** in the sidebar

### Step 2: Create New Post

Click the **"Create New"** button.

### Step 3: Fill in Required Fields

#### Title
A clear, descriptive title (will appear on the news grid):
- ‚úÖ "2024 SRI Poster Session"
- ‚úÖ "INSAR 2024 Conference"
- ‚ùå "News" (too vague)

#### Publication Date
Select the date for the post. This determines:
- Display order (newest first)
- The date shown on the post

#### Featured Image
The main image that appears on:
- The news grid
- Top of the full post
- Social media previews

**Image requirements:**
- Landscape orientation (wider than tall)
- Recommended: 1200x700px
- Aspect ratio: approximately 3:1.75
- JPEG or PNG format

#### Featured Image Description
Describe the image for accessibility:
- ‚úÖ "Lab members presenting posters at the 2024 SRI Poster Session"
- ‚ùå "Image" (too vague)

### Step 4: Write the Content

Use the rich text editor to write your post:

```markdown
The Muhle Lab had a fantastic showing at the 2024 SRI Poster Session at
Barnard College. Our undergraduate researchers presented their summer
research projects to faculty and students.

## Highlights

Our team presented research on:
- *CHD8* gene expression patterns
- Behavioral phenotyping of mouse models
- Single-cell RNA sequencing analysis

Congratulations to all presenters!

[Contact us](/contact) if you're interested in joining our team.
```

**Formatting options:**
- **Bold** for emphasis
- *Italics* for gene names
- Links to internal pages or external sites
- Headers (##) for sections
- Bullet points for lists

### Step 5: Add Image Carousel (Optional)

If you have multiple photos from an event:

1. Scroll to **Image Carousel** section
2. Click **"Add Image"**
3. Upload each image
4. Add a description for each
5. Repeat for all images

The carousel will appear at the bottom of the post with:
- Navigation arrows
- Automatic slideshow (5 seconds per image)
- Pagination dots

### Step 6: Set Publishing Options

| Option | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Published** | ‚úÖ Check to make visible on the site |
| **Featured on Homepage** | ‚úÖ Check to show in "Latest News" section |

**Tip:** You can create a draft by leaving "Published" unchecked. The post will only be visible in the admin panel until you publish it.

### Step 7: Save and Publish

1. Review all content
2. Click **"Save"**
3. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
4. Check the live site

## Post Templates

### Conference Presentation

```
Title: [Conference Name] [Year]

The Muhle Lab attended [Conference Name] in [City], where we presented
our latest research on [topic].

Dr. Rebecca Muhle presented a poster titled "[Poster Title]" which
covered [brief description].

We had valuable discussions with researchers from [institutions] about
[topics].

Looking forward to next year's conference!
```

### New Team Member

```
Title: Welcome [Name] to the Lab!

We're excited to welcome [Name] to the Muhle Lab as a [Role].

[Name] joins us from [Previous Institution/Background]. [He/She/They]
will be working on [research focus].

Welcome to the team, [First Name]!
```

### Award or Grant

```
Title: [Person/Lab] Receives [Award Name]

Congratulations to [Person] on receiving the [Award Name] from
[Organization]!

This [fellowship/grant/award] will support [research description]
for [duration].

[Quote from recipient if available]
```

### Publication

```
Title: New Publication in [Journal Name]

Our latest paper "[Paper Title]" has been published in [Journal Name].

## Key Findings

- Finding 1
- Finding 2
- Finding 3

Read the full paper: [Link to paper]

Authors: [Author list]
```

## Image Tips

### Carousel Images

For multi-image carousels (conferences, events):
- Use consistent aspect ratios
- Square (1:1) works well for carousels
- 800x800px minimum
- 5-10 images maximum

### Featured Images

For the main news image:
- Landscape orientation
- 1200x700px recommended
- Include people when possible
- Good lighting and composition

### Where to Get Images

- Lab members' phones (with permission)
- Conference photos (check usage rights)
- Stock photos from [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) (free)
- University media teams

## Managing Existing Posts

### Editing a Post

1. Go to News Posts
2. Click on the post title
3. Make changes
4. Save

### Removing from Homepage

To keep a post published but remove from "Latest News":
1. Open the post
2. Uncheck **"Featured on Homepage"**
3. Save

### Unpublishing a Post

To hide a post without deleting:
1. Open the post
2. Uncheck **"Published"**
3. Save

### Deleting a Post

1. Open the post
2. Click **"Delete"** (if available)
3. Or ask your webmaster

## Troubleshooting

**Post not appearing?**
- Check "Published" is enabled
- Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
- Clear browser cache

**Image not showing?**
- Check file format (JPEG/PNG)
- Check file size (under 5MB)
- Try re-uploading

**Carousel not working?**
- Ensure all images have descriptions
- Need at least 2 images
- Check image uploads completed
